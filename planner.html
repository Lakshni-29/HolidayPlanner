Seoul

— 4 nights
©
One of east Asia's major cultural and financial centers, Seoul boasts five World Heritage Sites and a huge selection of museums, temples, shrines, palaces, parks, gardens, and glass-and-steel skyscrapers.
Pack the 26th (Sat) with family-friendly diversions at Lotte World. And it doesn't end there: browse the exhibits of Gyeongbokgung Palace, take in nature's colorful creations at Namsan Park, take in panoramic vistas at N Seoul Tower, and wander the streets of Insadong.

Chennai, India to Seoul is an approximately 12-hour flight. You can also drive. Due to the time zone difference, you'll lose 3.5 hours traveling from Chennai to Seoul. Prepare for much colder weather when traveling from Chennai in February: high temperatures in Seoul hover around 5°C and lows are around -4°C. Finish your sightseeing early on the 1st (Tue) so you can fly to Busan.

Busan

— 1 night
©
The country's second city, Busan has often been referred to as South Korea's summer capital.
On the 2nd (Wed), contemplate in the serene atmosphere at Haedong Yonggungsa, kick back and relax at Haeundae Beach, and then stroll through Gwangalli Beach.

Traveling by flight from Seoul to Busan takes 2.5 hours. Alternatively, you can take a train; or drive. In March, daytime highs in Busan are 16°C, while nighttime lows are 3°C. Finish up your sightseeing early on the 2nd (Wed) so you can catch the flight to Jeju.

Jeju, Jeju Island

— 4 nights
©

Island of the Gods

The main town on the largest of Korea's islands, Jeju, has a volcanic landscape protected by UNESCO. The World Heritage-listed volcanic island includes the country's highest peak, sets of impressive lava tubes and columns, and several crater lakes.
Change things up with a short trip to Seongsan Ilchulbong in Seogwipo (about 1h 15 min away). And it doesn't end there: stroll around Sarabong Park, take in the pleasant sights at Handam Coastal Trail, explore the world behind art at Jeju Folk Museum, and take in nature's colorful creations at Hallim Park.

Traveling by flight from Busan to Jeju takes 2.5 hours. Alternatively, you can do a combination of car and ferry; or do a combination of train, taxi, and ferry. Wrap up your sightseeing by early afternoon on the 6th (Sun) to allow time for the flight back home.

South Korea travel guide

4.2
Shopping · History Museums · Parks
Land of the Morning Calm
Though it boasts a history spanning thousands of years, South Korea ranks among the world's most urbanized and modernized countries, with nearly half of its population living in high-rises concentrated in the national capital. In this land of pristine mountain scenery, many South Korea vacations involve some form of winter sports--not surprising for the country that won the bid to host the 2018 Winter Olympics. Curious travelers wishing to get to know South Korea's heart and soul spend only a small part of their holiday in big cities like Busan and Seoul, devoting much of their attention to the tranquil countryside that seems largely untouched by the 21st century.
